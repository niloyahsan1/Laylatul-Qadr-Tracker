Laylatul Qadr Checklist

This could be the night of power which is equal to 83 years (1000 months of worship). Complete the checklist below:

  • Astaghfirullah (100 times)
  • SubhanAllah (100 times)
  • Allahu Akbar (100 times)
  • Recite Surah Ikhlas (3 times)
  • Pray 2 Rakats Nafl Salah (as much as you can)
  • Read at least 1 page of the Quran (try more if you can)
  • Give charity (Even a small amount)

✔ Reflect & Repent: Seek forgiveness sincerely and make intentions for positive changes in life.

✔ Avoid Distractions: Stay away from unnecessary conversations, social media, or anything that takes time away from worship.
